What is Slobite?

Slobite is a community of talented local chefs in India. We help our chefs make a meaningful income by selling wholesome and healthy homemade meals in their locality.

Starting a traditional food business requires a lot of investment, manpower and technical requirements. We're about to change that. With Slobite, you can start a risk-free food delivery business from your home kitchen with no technical skills and zero administrative hassle.
